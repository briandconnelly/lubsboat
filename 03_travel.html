Airport

The nearest airport is Seattle-Tacoma International Airport (SEA). From the airport, you can get into the city using the Airporter Shuttle, Link light rail, or taxi/Uber/Lyft.

Getting Around Town

Seattle his very hilly and watery, so traveling just a few miles can take longer than you may be used to. Seattle has buses, light rail, and bikes. The most convenient option is usually to use ridesharing services like Uber and Lyft.

Places to Stay

A lot of tourists come to Seattle and the Pacific Northwest during the summer, so rooms tend to get booked quickly. These hotels are pretty close to the wedding venue. If you'd like to look at some other options, we'd recommend downtown. There are places outside of Seattle, but these could make getting around much more difficult.

Other Options

You can get some great deals when staying at other peoples' unoccupied houses, condos, and even houseboats and yachts through Airbnb and VRBO. The closest neighborhoods are Eastlake, Wallingford, and Fremont.
