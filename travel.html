Airport

The nearest airport is Seattle-Tacoma International Airport (SEA)

Getting Around Town

Because of the geography, traveling just a few miles can take longer than you may be used to. Seattle has buses, light rail, and bikes. The most convenient option is usually to use ridesharing services like Uber and Lyft.
